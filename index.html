Prevention from Corona Virus

stay home, stay safe

how to prevent

how to prevent virus

wash your place

Clean and disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces.

maintain distance

Maintain at least 1 meter (3 feet) distance between yourself and other people, particularly those who are coughing, sneezing and have a fever

don't touch face

Avoid touching your eyes, nose, and mouth with unwashed hands.

wash your hand

Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds. Use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol if soap and water are not available.

use napkin

Always use napkin. A napkin is more effective than conventional jet air dryers for removing microbes when drying poorly washed hands.

wear a mask

Always wear a mask. Masks can help prevent the spread of the virus from the person wearing the mask to others.

things you should DO

wash your hand

Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds. Use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol if soap and water are not available.

always wear a mask

Make wearing a mask a normal part of being around other people. The appropriate use, storage and cleaning or disposal of masks are essential to make them as effective as possible.

stay home during fever

Stay home and self-isolate even with minor symptoms such as cough, headache, mild fever, until you recover.If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical attention.

things you should NOT DO

avoid close contact with animals

Avoid contact with potentially contaminated animal waste or fluids on the soil or structures of shops and market facilities.Strictly avoid any contact with other animals in the market (e.g.stray cats and dogs, rodents, birds, bats).

avoid close contact with peoples

Avoid close contact with people who are sick.

avoid crowded area

Avoid crowded area.Avoid close contact with people who are sick.
